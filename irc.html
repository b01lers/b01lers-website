Port (SSL): 7000

How to connect to the b01lers channel on IRC

  • connect to freenode with the desired username
    • if you want to change your username then execute: 
      /nick YourNewNickname
  • Next register your account: 
    /msg nickserv register YourPassword YourEmail
  • Verify your email address once you receive an email
  • now that you are registered you will login and then verify your identity each time with: 
    /msg nickserv identify YourPassword
  • Connect to the channel #b01lers with: 
    /join #b01lers
